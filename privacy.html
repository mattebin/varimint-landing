VariMint — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

VariMint is a browser extension that helps Printify sellers bulk-edit the retail prices of their own products. It runs entirely in your browser using your own Printify Personal Access Token.

What data VariMint handles

Only your Printify Personal Access Token, which you enter yourself. It is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) so you don't have to re-enter it each session.

What we collect

Nothing. VariMint has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. The developer does not receive, see, or store your token, your shop data, or any personal information.

Who your data is shared with

Only Printify (api.printify.com). VariMint uses your token to call the Printify API on your behalf — to read your shops and products and to update your product prices when you choose to. No data is sent anywhere else.

Permissions

  • storage — to save your token locally on your device.
  • Access to api.printify.com — to make the Printify API calls that read and update your products.

Deleting your data

Use "Forget token" in the extension, clear the extension's storage, or uninstall VariMint — any of these removes your token from your browser. There is nothing stored anywhere else to delete.
